About Us

Who We Are

LDR AI LABS PRIVATE LIMITED is an AI-first EdTech startup founded in June 2026, incorporated in Chhattisgarh, India. We are building the next generation of personalized exam revision tools powered by Artificial Intelligence.

Our flagship product, LastDay Revision, is India's first AI-powered personalized exam revision platform — designed to help students revise any subject, for any university, in any professional course — in minutes.

We believe every student in India deserves access to high-quality, personalized revision tools — regardless of their background, income, or location.
